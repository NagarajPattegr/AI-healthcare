Three-Phase Integration Framework

Phase 1:AI Consultation

  • Objective: Understand business challenges, workflow bottlenecks, and desired outcomes through careful engagement with hospital leadership and department heads.
  • Process: Conduct stakeholder interviews, map existing processes, identify key pain points, and outline AI opportunities tailored to the institution.
  • Deliverable: A strategic roadmap detailing where AI can generate the greatest value—whether in diagnostics, administration, patient communication, or clinical support.
